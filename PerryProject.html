Finding Water in the Enigma Desert of Planet Chromatica

Client: The Kindness Punks

A Quest for Water and Peace

The planet Chromatica used to rot in conflict. Many tribes battled for dominance and resoursed in the great Enigma Desert. While the Spiritual ones prayed and slept for peace, only the Kindness Punks fought for peace in Chromatica, with a little help from III.

Led by their fearless leader - intergalatic superstar Lady Gaga, the Kindness Punks were on a mission to bring water to the scorched Enigma Desert to end all wars between the local tribes. They contacted our firm and with our expertise in GIS and remote sensing, we were able to locate various resources of water deep below the ground of the desert.
